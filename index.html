1950s — Birth of Rock & Roll

Rock and roll emerged from rhythm & blues, country, and gospel traditions. Teen culture and radio helped spread the new sound across America.

Key Artists

  • Elvis Presley
  • Chuck Berry
  • Little Richard

Important Songs

  • "Johnny B. Goode"
  • "Hound Dog"
  • "Tutti Frutti"
×

1960s — British Invasion & Psychedelia

The 1960s saw rapid musical experimentation, social change, and the global rise of rock bands.

Key Artists

  • The Beatles
  • The Rolling Stones
  • Jimi Hendrix

Important Songs

  • "Hey Jude"
  • "Purple Haze"
  • "(I Can't Get No) Satisfaction"

1970s — Arena Rock, Disco & Punk

Rock music expanded into many directions including stadium rock, disco dance culture, and punk rebellion.

Key Artists

  • Led Zeppelin
  • Fleetwood Mac
  • The Ramones

Important Songs

  • "Stairway to Heaven"
  • "Dreams"
  • "Blitzkrieg Bop"
